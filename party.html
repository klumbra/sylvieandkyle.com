Our Wedding Party

The guys

I met Adam shortly after I transferred to Boston University. He was one of the first friends I made in Boston and remains the closest. He's also the second-funniest person I know.

Corey is my cousin and one my earliest friends. I remember playing Sega Game Gear at his house and striving in vain to get past level 2 of Sonic. He has been schooling me at video games ever since. We're also both members of the Kilgore clan.

Josh is one of the smartest people I know, and a great racquetball partner. Both qualities have proved invaluable over the couse of our friendship. Ever since he joined the Bar I've had to fight the urge to call him "Counselor".

Jason and I have been close friends ever since his family moved to Essex when we were in middle school. Between snowboarding and driving around in my (trusty but ultimately ill-fated) Dodge Caravan, we've had at least a dozen near-death experiences.

The girls

I met Aubrey at the start of college. We met and bonded over our love of spinach flavored bagels during early morning Writing 101! Aubrey has been my rock ever since and we have shared an infinite number of laughs.

Jill has been one of my best friends ever since I moved into the house across the street from her 18 years ago! We keep each other grounded and I can always count on her for impromptu Just Dance marathons in the living room!

I’ve known Jen since the beginning of college. She has the biggest heart of anybody I’ve ever met. Of note however, her sweetness can be disarming as she is also one of the most competitive people I’ve ever known. We have spent many fun nights playing games like cribbage, pool, Citadels, and most recently (at the urging of Kyle and Adam) - Halo.

I met Ahri during high school while at summer camp for strange kids that prefer to spend their summers studying. We met and bonded instantly over a rare NH Korean connection! She is one of the most unknowingly funny people I’ve ever met and I have been so lucky to have her in my life (especially as her mom makes DELICIOUS korean food, and her dad owns the yummiest bakery).
