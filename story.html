Our Story

Some background information:

In 2009 Jen introduced Sylvie to Kyle with the intention of finding a suitable double date couple. Upon the initial meetup, Kyle fell for Sylvie, unbeknownst to her. Unfortunately, these feelings were not reciprocated. Several months passed, and in February of 2010, Sylvie and 7 friends (including Kyle) decided to go on a cruise for spring break. While on the ship, Sylvie spent more time with Kyle and fell for him.

Since then, Sylvie has been teaching Kyle how to cook, and Kyle has been teaching Sylvie how to clean. They make a good pair. In August 2012, while Kyle was in Chicago for work, he took a trip to the Jewelry District. After a long week, on a hot, humid day in August, Kyle convinced Sylvie (and Noodle) to hike Mount Piper. Halfway up the mountain, they heard thunder, and Sylvie tried to turn around. Kyle convinced Sylvie to continue to the top. At the top of the mountain he proposed, and now they are getting married!
